Frequently Asked Questions

What is this website for?

Boy Scouts of America is moving towards a paperless camp registration process. This website is designed to help you see camp availability, register for camps, and monitor your registration as you prepare for camp!

Where should I start?

Start by finding what camps are available. On the home page, you will see a list of all the camps, and a progress bar on the right side of the screen. That progress bar represents the camp availaility, for scouts only. Leaders, adults, and chiefs are not included in these numbers. The red color represents spots that are confirmed filled. The yellow portion represents pending positions. These are waiting for payment reciept before they turn red, and become confirmed. Pending reservations will "timeout" if they remain unpaid for a week, and the spots will becoe available once again. The green portion of the progress bar represents the outright availability of a camp!

Okay.... Now what?

Find a camp with adequate available space, and click the green button to regester for that camp! Its that easy!

Here's the exciting part. When you registered, you should have received an email confirmation. You also were automatically enrolled added to our website as a unique user. So login, and you will be re-directed to you registration page! Your password is automatically generated, and is included in that original confirmation email.
